About Us

Spark Trading Company Limited is a trusted supplier and distributor of high-quality electrical products, committed to powering progress across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. With a focus on safety, innovation, and performance, we offer a wide range of electrical components including cables, circuit breakers, lighting systems, switches, sockets, distribution boards, and control equipment.


Backed by a team of experienced professionals and partnerships with leading manufacturers, Spark Trading Company Limited delivers reliable solutions tailored to meet the demands of contractors, engineers, and developers. Whether for new installations, upgrades, or maintenance projects, we provide not only premium products but also expert technical support and prompt customer service.
