About Spark

We supply the systems behind safe, modern electrical work.

Spark Trading Company Limited is a trusted supplier and distributor of electrical products for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our range covers cables, circuit breakers, lighting systems, switches, sockets, distribution boards, and control equipment selected for dependable daily use.

We work with contractors, developers, facility teams, and technicians who need products that hold up in the field and a supplier who communicates clearly. That means practical recommendations, responsive service, and stock that supports both new installations and ongoing maintenance.

At Spark, we do not just supply electrical products. We deliver the power behind your projects.
